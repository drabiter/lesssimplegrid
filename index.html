LessSimpleGrid

LessSimpleGrid (LSG) is not next Bootstrap or Foundation, but something I need. While we human beings were born as unique entity, there's chance we share same taste so why don't share?

By "less" it means that this thing is a) built with less, b) not as simple as Simple-Grid, the original repository this's forked from, but still being simple.

Psst this is also inspired heavily by Kraken.
